Services

Consultation

You want technology to fit your life, not the other way around. Get expert advice that takes the hassle out of finding the right products for you. Talk to our in-store team of pros, tweet @twelpforce for answers 24/7, or use a variety of self-service tools to make buying easy.

Protection

With our Black Tie Protection plans and Buy Back program, there's no need to worry about your gear becoming damaged or dated. That means on-demand maintenance, ongoing tech support, and the assurance that you're always ahead of the technology curve. Put the power of Geek Squad at your command, and upgrade easily whenever new models arrive.

Install & Setup

Start enjoying your new electronics and appliances as soon as you get them home. Need help configuring a home theater? Setting up your wireless network? Or installing a navigation system? Just leave it to our technology SWAT team. Trust the Geek Squad to get your gear up and running quickly – and working just the way you want it – even if you bought it somewhere else.

Optimization

Are you more of the do-it-yourself type? Geek Squad Intelligence is the one-stop support and self-help resource for learning how to tackle technology issues on your own. Whatever issue you're trying to resolve, Geek Squad gives you the training, tips, and troubleshooting tools to make sure you get the most out of your products.

Our step-by-step guides make it easy to install your home theater or audio system.

Repair

No matter how careful you are, sometimes technology finds trouble. When things go wrong, you can trust your ailing electronics and appliances to our Healthy Tech expert repair service. An authorized service provider for most major brands, we provide in-home and remote repair services for free with an eligible warranty or service plan, or for a small fee when the warranty is expired.

Trade-In & Recycling

When you decide it's time for something new, we can help you give your old stuff a fresh start. Use our trade-in program to exchange your eligible used products for a Best Buy gift card, or bring in old electronics to make sure they get recycled properly and safely. We recycle almost anything electronic absolutely free, no matter where you bought it.
